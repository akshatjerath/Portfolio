Store Mode

Some pictures and video to give an insight of the story mode and detailing of the map played by a single player.

The game proceeds with the player who has been captured by an evil scientist and tries to find out ways to escape. The player hacks into an enemy robot and controls the system. The robot fights and destroys any resistance which comes in the way of his escape.

Tower Defense mode

Here are some pictures of how the map looks and a video of the tower defence game mode in a split screen multiplayer.

The third game mode is Tower Defense. The goal of the mode is to survive the enemy waves and score points by killing them off. Both the players must work together and need to defend themselves from enemy waves. If they survive all the waves with out either off them dying the win.
