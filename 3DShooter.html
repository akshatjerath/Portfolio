It was a part of my university course work. This was a framework given to us by the university. I was the one who worked on the level design and the stats of the game elements. My focus was to give the player a non-linear gameplay. After giving a few playtestings and different variations I removed the 2nd path and conected it to the main one. My idea was if the player goes from path 1 he shall face more difficulty enemies but the route to the boss room will be shortened, if he takes the 2nd path he has to take the longer route to reach the end.
